Our Mission

Campus Connect is a student-to-student platform built to foster a helpful, collaborative, and sustainable campus community.

By connecting students together, we make it simple to find lost belongings, share notes, buy/sell second-hand textbooks, and rent essentials without leaving the university grounds.

Core Features

  • 🔦 Lost & Found — Help reunite owners with their lost belongings quickly.
  • 📦 Buy & Sell — Trade textbooks, lab coats, drawing boards, and gadgets.
  • 🔑 Rentals — Borrow or lease items like calculators, laptops, and cycles.
  • 📢 Announcements — Stay up-to-date with club notices, speaker sessions, and workshops.
