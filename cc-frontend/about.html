About IET Connect

← Settings

IET Connect is a campus community platform built exclusively for students of IET DAVV to make everyday campus life easier, safer, and more connected. Our platform enables students to report lost and found items, rent or lend belongings, buy and sell used items, and communicate with fellow students—all in one secure place. By connecting students directly, IET Connect promotes collaboration, trust, and sustainability within the campus community.

Whether you've misplaced your ID card, want to rent a calculator for an exam, sell a used textbook, or find affordable essentials from classmates, IET Connect provides a simple and reliable solution.

Core Features

  • 🔦 Lost & Found — Help reunite owners with their lost belongings quickly.
  • 📦 Buy & Sell — Trade textbooks, lab coats, drawing boards, and gadgets.
  • 🔑 Rentals — Borrow or lease items like calculators, laptops, and cycles.
  • 📢 Announcements — Stay up-to-date with club notices, speaker sessions, and workshops.
