💡 You can post it here on Campus Connect first so the owner can contact you! Alternatively, you can drop it off at the security office near the main gate or the F-Block office.
❓ Is there a limit on how many rental items I can list?
Asked by LibraryReader • 5 days ago
💡 No, you can list as many items for sale or rent as you want. However, please make sure to mark them as completed/rented in "My Posts" once they are no longer available.
❓ How do I coordinate the transaction for buying items?
Asked by Fresher2026 • 1 week ago
💡 We recommend meeting up in public campus areas (like the Central Canteen or the Library foyer) during daylight hours to safely inspect items and coordinate payments.
